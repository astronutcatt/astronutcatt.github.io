Our Story

Swiss Precision.
Singapore Reach.

Swiss Ace was founded on a simple conviction: that complex global business and investment challenges deserve disciplined, research-driven solutions — delivered by advisors who have genuine operational experience, not just theoretical expertise.

Founded in Switzerland and registered in Singapore, we bring together European precision and Asian dynamism. Our Swiss heritage informs our approach to structure, governance, and long-term value creation. Our Singapore base gives us direct access to Asia's fastest-growing markets and capital flows.

Over more than a decade, we have grown into a trusted advisor for a diverse client base — from family offices and institutional funds to mid-market corporations and technology ventures — across more than 40 global markets.
